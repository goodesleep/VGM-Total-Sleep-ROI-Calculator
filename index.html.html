* 90-day compliance comes from the machine manufacturer's report. With order history, DME compliance at 180 / 270 / 365 days is predicted from the client's actual ordering rates (Ad Hoc Sales Order History Report) ÷ 0.89, a factor derived from analysis across many client datasets. Without order history, industry-average compliance (55% / 46% / 42% at a 73% 90-day baseline) is scaled proportionally to the client's 90-day compliance.
** Sanusom compliance curve, adherence, and retention improvements are based on historical averages across Sanusom partner DMEs. Sanusom software cost is included in the VGM fee.
*** Machine revenue is the average allowable collected for a compliant patient over the full rental term (enter what a machine pays out when adherence is met — not blended actuals, which already bake in denials). The portion earned after month 3 is treated as at-risk (contingent on documented adherence); the compliance uplift is applied only to that share. Revenue paid upfront or otherwise guaranteed regardless of adherence should be reflected by lowering the at-risk %. Default 77% mirrors the historical study split — lower it for payer mixes with more upfront-pay or shorter-term plans.
